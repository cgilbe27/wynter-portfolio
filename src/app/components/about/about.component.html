My Not-So-Secret Identity

The name is Wynter (yes, like the season) and I am a UX Designer. I am a people enthusiast which definitely explains my background in healthcare and degree in psychology. I have always had this creative fire that has led to writing short stories, exploring local museums, and my love for pop culture. UX has united my love for people and my creative passion into an actual career. Now here I am tapping into my empathetic nature to create playful, delightful, user-centered designs.

What I Believe

Good design comes down to knowing and understanding the user. As a designer, I have learned that when in doubt--ask the user. You just have to ask the right questions, which is where I come in! My psychology background (and my chattiness) has given me a solid foundation in conducting research and uncovering user insights. Good research results in good design. And establishing an empathetic connection with users? Priceless.
