Why Ontrak?

A few reasons why I am interested in being a part of the Ontrak team.

Reason one

While doing research on Ontrak, I learned that the approach used leads to 65% reduction in hospitization. I worked as a behavior health technician at a sizable, city hosptial. The fact that I have potential of working for a company that aids in the reduction of hospitalization is amazing. Proactive, preventative care is better than reactive care.

Reason two

Proof is in the pudding. Previous research has shown that 50% of clients stop going to therapy after their 3rd session. Ontrak has an 80% retention rate which is metaphorically and literally HUGE.

Reason three

From what I understand, Ontrak takes a personalized approach to care. Addressing the whole health of a member and the barriers is essiential to effective care which Ontrak has proven to be true (see reason one and two). When I worked in the hospital system, I felt like that was lacking. I saw time and time again where patients’ conditions were treated and not the patient themselves. I find it inspiring and admirable that Ontrak mission is to deliver a unique healthcare experience that is evidence based and person-focused.
